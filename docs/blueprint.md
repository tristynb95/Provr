# **App Name**: Provr Pulse

## Core Features:

- Pulse Check Survey: A brief, recurring survey to gauge employee sentiment on job aspects, workplace, peers, and leadership, including an Employee NPS question.
- Competency & General Feedback Surveys: Tools for detailed feedback and self-assessments covering staff competencies, behaviors, and attitudes (e.g., GAIL's SPARK competency survey).
- Self-Assessment Completion: Allows employees to complete any available survey as a self-assessment.
- Personalized Feedback Dashboard: A dedicated dashboard where users can view feedback received, review their self-assessments, and compare them side-by-side.
- Shower Thoughts Submission: A simple text field for staff to submit ideas, concerns, or any feedback to their manager, with options for anonymous or named submissions.
- Overview & Action Center: The main homepage displaying pending feedback requests, sections to initiate feedback requests (from specific individuals or anonymously from teams), and a quick link to post a 'Shower Thought'.
- AI-Powered Feedback Summarizer Tool: Utilize generative AI to provide concise summaries of extensive feedback, helping users quickly understand key themes, strengths, and areas for development from their received responses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, sophisticated red (#7A1F26) to reflect the GAIL's bakery branding, suggesting warmth and classic elegance.
- Background color: A very light, subtle reddish-grey (#EFEDED) to maintain a clean aesthetic while harmonizing with the primary red.
- Accent color: Dark charcoal with a subtle red undertone (#282525) to provide strong contrast for text and key interactive elements, reflecting the 'black' component of the brand.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly techy, and precise feel, enhancing the 'slick' and 'internal tool' aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high legibility and a clean, neutral look, suitable for extensive feedback text and survey content.
- Utilize minimalist, line-art style icons that are easily recognizable and contribute to a modern, uncluttered interface.
- A responsive, fluid grid system ensures optimal readability and interaction on both mobile devices and larger screens, featuring ample whitespace for a clean and easy-to-navigate experience.
- Subtle, smooth animations for transitions, button hovers, and feedback submission confirmations to create a more interactive and polished user experience without being distracting.
